Course Reflection

I personally enjoyed this course, though I feel it has a few shortcomings. However, those can easily be brushed aside since it’s a new class. Throughout this semester, my knowledge of live TV and interviews has grown exponentially. I now have a much better understanding of the video process — not just the editing side, but the full process of lighting, cameras, setups, and scripts.
